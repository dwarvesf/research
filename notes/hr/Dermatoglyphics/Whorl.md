Eagle type (Whorl) accounts for 30-35% (9 types): has a swirl pattern and 2 intersections

- Plain Whorl (WT, WS, WE) = 24%,
- Central Pocket/ Peacock eye (WP, WL) = 2%,
- Double Loop/Composite (WC, WD, WI) = 4%,
- Accidental (WA) = 0.01%.
